Andy's Life!!!

When a house of love,
Get's torn away from you,
Storms in your heart will brew,
Filling your mind with teardrops.

With hair that resembles acorns,
A spotty jumper complete with mulberry stains,
This tall teenage girl,
Has jeans ripped by the knees.

Her thoughts filled with anger and fear,
She wants to go back to the cottage she holds dear,
In her eyes are sparks full with determination,
To sow the rip in her family back together.
